Overview

Collaboration with MIT and Amazon Web Services, this summer camp is all about empowering young women. Led by women in engineering, including myself, we taught classes that brought computational thinking, software engineering, and coding to life.
Through hands-on learning in algorithms, problem-solving, and coding, we inspired participants to see the endless possibilities of learning, growth, and innovation.
